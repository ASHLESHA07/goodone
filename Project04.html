KHATU SHYAM DARBAR

Khatu Shyam Darbar is a famous temple in Rajasthan.And the story of Khatu Shyam ji.It is a pilgrimage site for worshipping the deity Krishna and Barbarika who is often venerated as a Kuladevata. Devotees believe the temple houses the head of Barbarika or Khatushyam, a legendary warrior who sarcrifices his head upon the request of Krishna during the antebellum of the Kurukshetra War.
